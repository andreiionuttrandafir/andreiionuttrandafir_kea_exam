Exmouth View Hotel is a detached, modern Hotel which occupies one of the finest positions in Babbacombe.
On a corner of beautiful Babbacombe Downs, the Hotel is central in a quiet select area away from traffic noise.
Immediately below the Downs are Babbacombe and Oddicombe Beaches offering safe and superb facilities.
The Model Village, which is a must for any visitor to Torquay, is just a gentle stroll from the Hotel, as are the Theatre, Golf Club, Bygones, Kents Cavern and Cary Park with its Tennis, Putting and Bowling Greens.
Torquay town centre, with its lively night life and busy shopping centre, is just five minutes away by car or bus.
The Hotel has ample parking for all guests' cars.
